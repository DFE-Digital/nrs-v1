Challenge 4

Proportions to percentages

View the answer

Answer:

162 people were against the proposal.

Working out:

Since 36 per cent of the 450 people were against the proposal, you want to calculate 36 per cent of 450 to find the actual number. Here's two methods you could have used.

1. Percentage against:

36% × 450 = 36⁄100 × 450 = 162

2. Using a decimal instead of a fraction:

36% × 450 = 0.36 × 450 = 162
