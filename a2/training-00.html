Hospitality manager training course

There are 3 ways to do this high quality training course designed to fit your time and budget. The course leads to a qualification that employers value.

Option 1 - Online only

  • 6 months
  • £99.00 (Financing Available)

Study anytime, anywhere. The course is accessible 24 hours a day 7 days a week to fit in around your lifestyle. The course includes video tutorials and your course tutor is available to answer your questions.

More information

Option 2 - Online + classroom

  • 3 months
  • £250.00 (Financing Available)

This course is a mix of online learning where you can study anytime, anywhere + Classes at your local colleges where you can work with your tutor and other students face-to-face

More information

Option 3 - Bootcamp

  • 2 weeks
  • £1000.00 (Financing Available)

This is an intensive, two-week course at your local college where you study every day. By studying intensively you build skills much faster.
